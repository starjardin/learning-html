How to write html quicker

There are many ways to write html much quicker. These are some tips for writing html.

  • Avoid using unnecessary element.
  • Don't use your mouse instead use keyboard shortcut
  • Make a template of your html.
  • Use tabs

How to make sure your work is high quality

You can do your best. These are some tips for writing high quality html.

  • Ask your colleagues to review your work.
  • Avoid using unnecessary elements.
  • Don't use css or any styles inside html tags.
  • Follow the rules of the elements.
  • Plan before you write.
  • Spell correctly your words.
  • Take coding challenges.
  • Test your work in data validator
  • Verify your work.
  • Write your code as simple as possible.
